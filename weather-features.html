Weather Features

This page outlines the features of the Weather App and how they can be utilized.

  • Current Weather: Displays the current temperature, humidity, wind speed, and weather conditions.
  • 5-Day Forecast: Provides a detailed forecast for the next 5 days, including temperature and weather conditions every 3 hours.
  • Weather Icons: Icons are used to visually represent different weather conditions.
  • Date Navigation: Allows users to navigate through dates to view historical or future weather data.
